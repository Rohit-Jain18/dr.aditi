My story — honestly

I didn't start out
with clear skin either.

Growing up, I struggled with severe scarring acne, atopic dermatitis, PCOS-related hair fall, and the kind of skin that made me avoid mirrors and skip photos. I spent years hiding under full-coverage foundation, cancelling plans, and spiralling from one failed "solution" to the next.

That struggle is actually what pushed me to become a dermatologist — I needed the answers myself, first. I studied the science deeply, trained under exceptional mentors, and slowly — very slowly — figured out what actually works for skin like mine. It wasn't one product, one peel, or one miracle. It was understanding root cause, building a protocol, and giving it time.

I'm sharing this because I know exactly how isolating that cycle feels. And because I want you to know: there is a way through. It just takes the right guidance, a realistic plan, and someone who's walked the path themselves.
